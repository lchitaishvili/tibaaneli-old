2015

Chardonnay

  • Denomination: Doc Wine
  • Grape: White

Tasting notes:

It has an aromatic profile where delicate nuances of vanilla, butter and hazelnut emerge, accompanied by a refined minerality. In the mouth, it stands out for its freshness and uniqueness, with a soft structure and a persistent finish that invites a subsequent sip.

Recommended pairings:

Excellent with fish-based dishes, such as baked sea bass or a selection of shellfish, it perfectly enhances the marine notes.

2010

Cabernet Pinot

  • Denomination: Doc Wine
  • Grape: Red

Tasting notes:

Wine that presents itself with an intense red color, with purple hues. On the nose, it offers a complex bouquet in which aromas of berries, such as currants and blueberries, intertwine, accompanied by delicate herbaceous notes and spices. In the mouth it reveals itself to be structured and harmonious, with soft tannins.

Recommended pairings:

It pairs perfectly with red meat dishes, such as a succulent grilled fillet or braised beef with red wine sauce.

2005

Sangiovese

  • Denomination: Doc Wine
  • Grape: Red

Tasting notes:

Red wine of great character, typical of the Italian winemaking tradition. It has an intense ruby ​​red color, often with garnet reflections in older wines. On the nose, it offers a complex and fascinating bouquet, with hints of ripe cherry, plum, and floral notes of violet. On the palate, it is harmonious and well structured.

Recommended pairings:

It pairs well with red meats, roast beef, lamb, tomato-based dishes, pasta or lasagna.
